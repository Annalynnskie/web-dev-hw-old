Pavlova

Ingredients

  • egg whites
  • sugar
  • an acid such as cream of tartar or vinegar
  • cornstarch
  • vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix ingredients in stand mixer
  3. Beat into stiff peaks
  4. Spread mixture into a circle on a lined baking pan
  5. Start baking the Pavlova at 350°F then reduce the heat to 200°F
  6. Bake for 90 min or until it's firm and dry
  7. Let cool in oven
  8. Top with whipped cream and fruit
