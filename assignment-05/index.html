Pavlova

Ingredients

  • egg whites
  • sugar
  • an acid such as cream of tartar or vinegar
  • cornstarch
  • vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Mix ingredients in stand mixer
  2. Beat into stiff peaks
  3. Spread mixture into a circle on a lined baking pan
  4. Start baking the Pavlova at 350°F then reduce the heat to 200°F
  5. Let cool in oven
  6. Top with whipped cream and fruit
