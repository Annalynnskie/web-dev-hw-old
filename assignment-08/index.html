Designing a Character

Materials

Choose any medium

  • Graphite
  • Charcoal
  • Paint
  • etc...

Directions

  1. Make several small sketches of your character (thumbnail sketches)
  2. Mark the ones you like best and redraw them larger
  3. Create variations of those drawings
  4. Think in terms of shape/silhouette
  5. Think about backstory and see if you can incorporate it into the design or if the design makes sense with it
  6. Draw the character in various poses/situations
  7. Don't get too attached things could still change
