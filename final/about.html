About

Anna Selinger

Anna is an artist who primarily works with 2D mediums like painting, drawing and printmaking. She also enjoys creating digital art and small animations. Currently she is learning 3D modeling in Blender. She will be a second time senior at the University of Montana next schoolyear and is working towards getting a Bachelors of Fine Art, a Media Arts Minor, and a Digital Design Certificate. She does digital art commissions in her free time and aspires to work in game design in the future. When she's not at school she resides in Delaware, where her family live.
